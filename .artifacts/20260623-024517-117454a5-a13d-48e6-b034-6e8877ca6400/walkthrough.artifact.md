# WACC Calculator Implementation Walkthrough

I have implemented a **"Professional+" WACC (Weighted Average Cost of Capital) Calculator**. Recognizing that this is a complex financial tool, I have prioritized **simplicity and guidance** to ensure you can use it effectively even without a finance background.

## 🚀 What's New

### 1. The WACC Strategy Dashboard
- **2-in-1 Logic**: Includes an integrated **CAPM (Cost of Equity)** calculator. You enter basic risk factors (like Beta), and it determines your cost of equity automatically.
- **Tax Shield Savings**: A unique feature that shows you exactly how much money your company saves in taxes every year due to debt interest deductions.
- **Visual Capital Structure**: A dynamic bar that shows your company's balance between Equity and Debt.
- **Location**: [/finance/wacc-calculator](file:///M:/techprojects/UtilitiesSite/apps/hilmost_toolbox_web/src/app/finance/wacc-calculator/page.tsx).

### 2. Built-in "How-to-Use" Guide
- **Simplistic 3-Step Guide**: Embedded directly on the tool's page to explain exactly what to enter in each section.
- **Glossary Tooltips**: Hover over any label (like "Beta" or "Risk-Free Rate") for a plain-English explanation of what that number means and where to find it.

### 3. Knowledge Base Article
- **Title**: "Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC): A Beginner's Guide."
- **Content**: Explains WACC as your "Hurdle Rate"—the minimum return you need to justify any new project or investment.
- **Location**: [knowledge-base.ts](file:///M:/techprojects/UtilitiesSite/packages/config/src/knowledge-base.ts).

### 4. Site-Wide Integration
- **Homepage Visibility**: Moved to the **Top 1** position in the Finance category on the homepage.
- **Finance Hub**: Fully integrated with SEO-optimized Metadata and Breadcrumb Schema.
